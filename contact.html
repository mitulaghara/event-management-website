Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services.

How far in advance should I book my event?

+

We recommend booking at least 3-6 months in advance for weddings and large events, and 1-2 months for smaller gatherings. However, we can accommodate last-minute requests depending on availability.

What is included in your event management services?

+

Our comprehensive services include event planning, venue selection, vendor management, decor and theme design, catering coordination, entertainment booking, and on-site event management. We offer customized packages based on your specific needs.

Do you handle events outside of Gujarat?

+

Yes, we manage events across India. Additional travel and accommodation costs may apply for events outside Gujarat, which will be discussed during the planning phase.

Can you work within my budget?

+

Absolutely! We believe in creating memorable events regardless of budget size. We'll work with you to prioritize elements and create an event that fits your financial plan while delivering maximum impact.
